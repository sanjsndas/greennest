Cookies Policy

Welcome to GreenNest Care’s Cookies Policy page. Cookies and similar technologies are fundamental to providing you with an optimized browsing experience and tailored services. This policy outlines the types, purposes, and your choices regarding cookies used on our website.

We encourage you to review all points carefully to gain comprehensive understanding of how cookies affect your visit and how we protect your privacy in accordance with global standards.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit. They store information about your preferences, login status, and site activity, enabling improved functionality and personalization during subsequent visits.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Our website uses several cookie types including essential cookies necessary for site functioning, performance cookies to monitor usage, functionality cookies to remember preferences, and targeting/advertising cookies to deliver relevant content and ads.

3. Essential Cookies

These cookies enable core functions like page navigation, login, and access to secure areas. Without these, the website cannot operate effectively, and disabling them may limit your ability to use certain features.

4. Performance and Analytics Cookies

We utilize performance cookies to collect anonymous information about site usage, such as pages visited, time spent, and error reports. This data helps us understand user behavior and improve the website’s speed, reliability, and content.

5. Functionality Cookies

These cookies remember your choices such as language preferences, font sizes, and region, offering enhanced and personalized experiences without requiring repeated input.

6. Advertising and Targeting Cookies

Advertising cookies collect information about your browsing habits to present relevant and personalized ads both on our website and on third-party platforms. We only use these with your consent and comply with applicable regulations.

7. How We Use Cookies

Cookies are used to optimize site functionality, recognize repeat visitors, store your preferences, analyze traffic, and deliver personalized content and offers aimed to enhance your overall experience with GreenNest Care.

8. Third-Party Cookies

Our site may include cookies set by third parties such as analytics providers and advertising networks. These third-party cookies follow their own privacy and data policies. We encourage you to review their policies for detailed information.

9. Your Consent

Upon visiting GreenNest Care, you will be presented with a cookie consent dialog. You may accept or decline cookies or customize preferences. Your consent choices will be respected and stored for subsequent visits to reduce repeated prompts.

10. Managing and Disabling Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser’s settings at any time. Disabling certain cookies may prevent full functionality of the site. We provide detailed guides on cookie management for popular browsers on request.

11. Essential Cookies Technical Details

Essential cookies typically have short lifespans and do not track user behavior beyond session duration. They ensure security in transactions and enable navigation between service pages.

12. Analytics Cookies Usage

We analyze aggregate data from performance cookies to identify high-traffic pages, user navigation flow, and technical errors to continually refine user experience across devices and geographies.

13. Advertising Cookies Transparency

Targeted advertising cookies may create anonymous profiles based on your browsing history. We strictly adhere to privacy norms and do not collect sensitive personal identifiable data through these means.

14. Cookie Duration and Lifespan

Cookies can be session-based or persistent. Session cookies expire at the end of your visit; persistent cookies remain for set durations to recall your preferences.

15. Impact of Cookie Decline

If you decline cookies, you may experience reduced personalization, repeated prompts, and limited access to certain functionalities. Core services will remain accessible as essential cookies are strictly necessary.

16. Cookie Settings Consent Storage

Your cookie preferences are stored securely and used only to determine what cookies are deployed on your device during web visits, helping comply with privacy regulations.

17. Use of Local Storage

Besides cookies, our website may use local storage technologies to save your preferences and settings across sessions in a secure manner enhancing user experience.

18. Integrations with Third Parties

Third-party services such as Google Analytics, social media widgets, and advertising platforms may use cookies on our site in accordance with their privacy policies.

19. Security of Cookies

We enforce security practices including HTTPS protocol, secure flags on cookies, and regular reviews to protect cookie integrity and prevent misuse or interception.

20. Updates to Cookie Policy

This Cookies Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in regulatory requirements or our operational practices. We recommend reviewing this policy regularly.

21. User Rights Concerning Cookies

You have rights to revoke consent, manage cookie preferences, and request access or deletion of personal data collected via cookies where applicable under law.

22. Cookie Policy Contact Information

If you have questions about our cookie practices, please contact us at contact@GreenNestcare.com or phone +1 (833) 444-6690.

23. Compliance with Regulations

Our use of cookies adheres to GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable laws to ensure respectful, lawful processing of your data obtained by browser technologies.

24. Behavioral Advertising Disclosure

Cookies used for behavioral advertising may profile aggregate interests but do not reveal personal identities to third parties.

25. Opt-Out Mechanisms

You may opt out from cookie tracking for marketing purposes via browser extensions, opt-out platforms, or our cookie dialog interface at any time.

26. Cookies and Mobile Devices

Cookies function similarly on mobile devices through mobile browsers and apps. We ensure consistent privacy protection across device types.

27. Persistent vs. Session Cookies

Session cookies last only during your visit and delete after closing your browser; persistent cookies last as per defined expiration to enhance return visits.

28. Third-Party Advertising Servers

Third-party servers may deposit cookies to display customized advertising based on your browsing. We do not control the data collected by these providers.

29. Cookie Policy Scope

This policy applies only to cookies used on GreenNest Care's website and does not extend to external websites linked therein.

30. How to Delete Cookies

You can delete cookies via browser settings. Instructions vary by browser; consult help resources for details on clearing stored cookies and data.

31. Managing Consent Preferences

The cookie consent dialog appearing on your first visit allows setting preferences momentarily or permanently through your browser for recurring visits.

32. Impact on User Experience

Disabling cookies may limit functionality such as session persistence, personalized content, and certain interactive features.

33. Accessibility of Cookie Consent

Our cookie consent dialog is accessible via keyboard, screen readers and conveys information clearly for all visitors.

34. Information Collected by Cookies

Cookies can collect data such as IP addresses, browser types, device identifiers, browsing history, and site interaction details.

35. Use of Cookies in Marketing

Cookies assist in delivering relevant promotional content and measuring the efficacy of marketing campaigns to improve user targeting.

36. Cookie Consent Withdrawal

You have the right to withdraw your cookie consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings or using available opt-out options provided within the website.

37. Do Not Track Signals

Our website respects Do Not Track browser signals and adjusts cookie deployment accordingly where technically feasible.

38. Data Retention Related to Cookies

Data collected via cookies is retained only as long as necessary and deleted or anonymized in compliance with data retention policies.

39. Legal Basis for Cookie Usage

Where applicable, cookie usage is based on your informed consent or legitimate interests needed to provide website functionality and security.

40. Updates to Cookie Policy

This Cookies Policy may be updated periodically. We recommend revisiting this page for the latest information about how GreenNest Care manages cookies and your privacy.
